Hit the snow.

With multiple mountains nearby and all of them offering student passes , there's no reason not to go.

Whistler : About 3 hours away, Whistler is a premier destination for snow enthusiasts around the world.

There is also a bus service that can take you up and back for relatively cheap. (Maybe save this for sponsored section)Epic Rides

Grouse : A small resort that is only 2 hours away by bus. Recommended for those on a budget or just learning.

Cypress : A decent sized resort with two medium sized mountains. Intermediate and beginner terrain.

Cypress also has a couple buses that pick up from multiple locations in Vancouver. Cypress Coach Lines

Seymour : Another small resort that is great for those just learning.
