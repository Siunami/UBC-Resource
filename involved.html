Get Involved

Find something you love. Make friends.

Be willing to say yes.

There are so many ways to get involved starting the moment you arrive on campus.

Research

If research is something that interests you, then try to get into it as soon as possible. Researchers are looking for people who are capable, passionate and willing to learn.

A list of all the research centers are listed here.

If you find one that you would like to work at. Look up the directors of the center and read some of the work they have done. Then send an email to them and show that you're interested and looking to volunteer.

Recreation

Have fun and make friends

Intramurals: Find some friends and make a team to play in a wide variety of different sports at different competitive levels.

Storm the Wall: The most popular event of the year. Teams compete in a relay consisting of swimming, sprinting, running, biking and ending with the entire team scaling a wall together.

Day of the Longboat: Race around Jericho Beach in a canoe with 9 other people. Don't miss this.

Work

UBC provides so many opportunities for students to work. Take advantage of them.

Work Learn: A UBC initiative for students to gain valuable experience and skills on campus while studying full-time.

Internships: There are plenty of opportunities to build experience locally and internationally in every field.

Co-op: Although not intially available, for upper years, UBC helps place students in paid posistions in their field. Many times, this leads to full-time positions after university.

CareersOnline: A portal to many positions on and off campus such as notetakers for certain classes, administrative roles, and companies looking to hire students.

Clubs

UBC is home to over 300 different clubs.

The Big List Of Clubs

If you can find one or two to stick with, the friends you make will be very rewarding.

Residence Life

A lot of these events may seem boring but try to go to some especially at the beginnning of the year. It's one of the best ways to find friends because everyone is there for that reason. It may be a little uncomfortable at first but the people you meet there will likely be your close friends.
